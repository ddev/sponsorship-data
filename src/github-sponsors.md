## About the Team

### Randy Fay, Project Lead

Randy has been stewarding DDEV's development for over 8 years, working alongside an incredible community of contributors. Through conferences, workshops, and direct community support, Randy ensures DDEV remains accessible to developers at every skill level while pushing the boundaries of what local development can be.

### Stas Zhuk, Maintainer

Stas is a core maintainer of DDEV and creator/maintainer of numerous DDEV add-ons. Based in Kremenchuk, Ukraine, he became an official full-time DDEV maintainer in October 2023 after contributing to the project since 2021. With expertise in Laravel, PHP, and DevOps tools, he has contributed over 500 commits, bugfixes, and features to DDEV, including major improvements like enhanced composer commands, environment file support, and add-on ecosystem development. His deep technical knowledge and passion for innovation make him essential to DDEV's continued growth and sustainability.
